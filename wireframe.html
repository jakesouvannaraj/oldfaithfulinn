The Original Inn at Old Faithful, Spared by the skills of firefighters in the great blaze that razzed a good portion of Yellowstone Park. The Inn shows off craftsmanship no longer seen today. The rustic log structure that withstands year after year of heavy snow loads with very little modern structural reinforcement, is a testament to the skills of the designer and the craftsmen that assembled this Inn from Local Timbers and Stones.
